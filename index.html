About Me.

I am Prannvat Singh, a dedicated and product‐focused iOS developer with a passion for creating intuitive and user‐friendly applications. In this role, I have been responsible for designing and implementing key features, optimizing performance, and ensuring a seamless user experience. I have worked extensively with AVFoundation to handle multimedia data, and have leveraged SwiftUI to create dynamic and interactive user interfaces. Throughout my career, I have consistently stayed up‐to‐date with the latest iOS development trends and best practices, and have gained experience with a wide range of tools and technologies, including Xcode, CocoaPods, and various third‐party libraries. I am adept at writing clean, efficient, and maintainable code, and have a strong understanding of design patterns, data structures, and algorithms.
